Instructions And Rules to be followed

  • The game can be played by two players against each other.
  • Initially each player will be having a total 10000 points
  • There are 20 places where the players can increse the points or may lose the points.
  • Each place as mentioned in the box has some points.
  • Each player has to give one another equal number of turns to play.
  • Whenever the player lands on start place. 500 points to be added to player's Score as entry bonus.
  • Whenever the player lands on Bonus place.2000 points to be added to player's score as Bonus.
  • Whenever the player lands on Jail,Club, and Rest Home, 800 points will be deducted from the player's score.
  • If the player lands on Other places, following points each will be deducted from the player's score .
